Principles

Design principles are a set of guidelines our teams can follow throughout our design process in order to create more aligned experiences.

Design principles create alignment, speed up decision-making, and increase of the quality of our team's output.

Effective principles should be:

  • Universal and specific
  • Decision-making tools

Purpose statement

Primary
What is the goal?
Our design system will free up more time for innovation and creativity.
Why it is important?
If we don't increase our innovation, our website, docs, and portal could grow stagnant and be surpassed by competitors.
How will it help us?
By resolving common, recurring situations with repeatable patterns so Marketing, Docs, and Portal teams can focus on new problems.
Who is it for?
Our internal Marketing, Docs, and Portal teams.
