Composition

The testimonals section is made up of the following elements:

  1. Icon or lead: Optional
  2. Headline - H2: A primary heading and it should be sentence case.
  3. Message: A summary of the feature or the new product / service.
  4. Text link: Leads to its page. The action should be self-describing action verbs.
  5. Illustration: Should be composed of metaphorical imagery that relates to the content or benefit.
