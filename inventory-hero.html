Hero

Composition

The hero section is made up of the following elements:

  1. Lead: Page title or its category
  2. Headline - H1: A primary heading and it should be Sentence Case. The title provides a concise overview of the contents of the journey.
  3. Message: The short summary of the page.
  4. Buttons: Contains a maximum of two centered buttons. A primary action and secondary action. The action should be self-describing action verbs.
  5. Illustration: Should be composed of metaphorical imagery that relates to the content or benefit.

What is the goal?
Our design system will free up more time for innovation and creativity.
Our design system will make the quality of our products better for our audiences.
Why it is important?
If we don't increase our innovation, our website, docs, and portal could grow stagnant and be surpassed by competitors.
If our audiences are met with confusing, inconsistent workflows, we'll lose their trust.
How will it help us?
By resolving common, recurring situations with repeatable patterns so Marketing, Docs, and Portal teams can focus on new problems.
By defining and unifying a cohesive design patterns through shared tools, guidelines, and principles.
Who is it for?
Our internal Marketing, Docs, and Portal teams.
Our internal Marketing, Docs, and Portal teams, in service of our audiences.
