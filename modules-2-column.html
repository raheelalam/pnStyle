Inventory audit

Auditing inventory is to understand the current state of our design language and user interfaces. We're evaluating the quality and amount of variants in our current design as well.

2-column

Composition

The basic 2-column section is made up of the following elements:

  1. Title: Sentence Case. The title for this feature provides a concise overview of the feature of this product/service.
  2. Message: The short summary of the feature. It can include inline hyperlinks that entice the readers to dig deeper into our Docs or other relevant product pages.
  3. Illustration: Should be composed of metaphorical imagery that relates to the content or benefit.

Composition

The accordions 2-column section is made up of the following elements:

  1. Illustration: Should be composed of metaphorical imagery that relates to the content or benefit.
  2. Indicator: It not just tells how many slides are, also it responds to the transition of the copy together with the illustrations.
  3. Title: Sentence Case. The title for this feature provides a concise overview of the feature of this product/service. It should be grouped with Message, set to be clickable, and triggers the indicator/imageries transitioning.
  4. Message: The short summary of the feature. It can include inline hyperlinks that entice the readers to dig deeper into our Docs or other relevant product pages. It should be grouped with its Title, set to be clickable, and triggers the indicator/imageries transitioning.
