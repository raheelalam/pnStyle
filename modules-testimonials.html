Modules

Modules are reusable combinations of patterns that present well-structured information with a perfect balance between copy and imagery. These best practice solutions help users find the information efficiently, encourage the conversation, and help ensure consistency across experiences.

Testimonials

Composition

The testimonials section is made up of the following elements:

  1. Imagery: The image should reflects the industry. It is muted with 70% opacify in #000000.
  2. Customer's logo: In white.
  3. Indicator: It tells how many of testimonials, and it also interacts with each individual testimonial once it is clicked.
  4. Quote: It comes directly from our customer. Recommended length would be 4 lines.
  5. Name and job title
  6. Text link: Leads to its case study. The action should be self-describing action verbs.

Best practice

  1. Testimonials are the driving force behind purchasing decisions. They create immediate trust with prospective customers.
  2. Each use case or solution page should reserve a section for testimonials to gain the trust.

Images and logos

  1. Sometimes it is hard to grab a quality photo from the customer's website, therefore, we might find a stock photo that represents the industry or use case.
  2. Treatment: black out the image with 70% #000000 opacity and place the customer's logo in white horizontally and vertically centered on top of the treated image.

Content guidelines

Quote

  1. Quote should directly comes from the customer. It should focus on the benefits and the key advantages of why choose PubNub as the solution provider is the right decision by addressing customer's pain points.

Text link labeling

  1. Links should always lead with a strong verb that encourages action.
  2. Link labeling need to be clear and predictable.
  3. Always write the labeling in sentence case.
  4. Link labeling need to be clear and predictable.
